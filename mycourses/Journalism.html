Course Curriculum

The study of the principles, practices, and ethics of journalism, including the history and evolution of the field.

The study of the journalist's role in society, including the responsibilities and obligations to inform and engage the public.

The study of the principles of writing news stories, including structure, style, and tone, as well as research and reporting techniques.

The study of techniques for conducting interviews, including research and preparation, questioning strategies, and building rapport with interviewees.

The study of the impact of technology on journalism, including the use of social media, online news sources, and digital storytelling techniques.

The study of ethical principles and considerations in journalism, including objectivity, accuracy, and responsibility to the public.

The study of current trends and challenges facing the journalism industry, including changes in media consumption habits, new technologies, and the role of journalism in a changing society.
