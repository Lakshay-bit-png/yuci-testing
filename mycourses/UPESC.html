Course Curriculum

An overview of the Indian Civil Services, including the history and evolution of the system, eligibility criteria, and selection process.

The study of strategies and techniques for preparing for the preliminary exam, including study materials, time management, and test-taking strategies.

The study of strategies and techniques for preparing for the mains exam, including essay writing, answer writing, and time management.

The study of the Indian political system, including the Constitution, central and state government structures, and administrative processes.

The study of Indian history and culture, including major events, movements, and personalities that have shaped the country's development.

The study of Indian geography, including physical, economic, and cultural aspects of the country's geography.

The study of the Indian economy, including economic planning, growth, and development, and major economic policies.

The study of current events, issues, and developments of national and international importance, including politics, economics, society, and culture.

The study of basic principles of science and technology, including biology, physics, chemistry, and their application in everyday life.

The study of various optional subjects and how to select the most suitable one based on interests and career goals.

The study of techniques and strategies for writing effective essays, including organization, structure, and argumentation.

The study of techniques and strategies for preparing for the interview round of the selection process, including communication skills, body language, and presentation.

The study of ethical principles, integrity, and aptitude required to serve as a civil servant, including topics such as accountability, transparency, and public service values.
